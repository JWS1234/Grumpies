What's On It?

"

Below is the 2025 Calendar for The Grumpy Old Men Golf Club tournaments. It is a revolving calendar showing 52 weeks beginning in January and ending 52 weeks later in December. If it is currently June, then the calendar will show the rest of the current year and the first 5 months of the next year - but January is always at the top and December at the bottom. Many weeks will not have been scheduled but there is slot for them on the calendar. Should be obvious when you look at it.

Note that each event has an ID which consists of either an M (for Monday) or W (for Wednesday) followed by a 2-digit number representing the week. For example, M02 would be Monday of the 2nd week and W02 would be Wednesday of the 2nd week. The IDs repeat themselves the following year.

Players on the Every Monday and/or Every Wednesday List are automatically signed up. If you are not on this the Every list, you must sign up by clicking on the Event ID of an OPEN event (blue letters, the others are red - background color changes to yellow when you hover over it). You will be presented with an email form addressed to Grump Headquarters. Fill in the requested information and send it in. Note that if your email already contains your name and phone number, you don't need to re-enter it.
