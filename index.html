About The Grumpy Old Men

Who We Are

The Grumpy Old Men Golf Club was formed with the noble goal of getting grumpy senior golfers (age 55 or older) together twice a week to play golf without having to join a club ($$$) or break in with a group of golfers of similar age and skill.

A small group of volunteers does the grunt work of reserving tee times at area courses, collecting entry fees, organizing flights, groups, tee times and starting hole assignments, analyzing and posting the results, and paying out the winners. All the rest of us do is show up on time every Monday and Wednesday morning and TEE IT UP!

When We Play

We play golf every Monday and Wednesday throughout the year except when it conflicts with a holiday or bad weather. Shotgun tee times are early in the morning depending on the time of year - from 7:30 in the summer up to 9:00 or later as the days get shorter and the weather gets colder.

Where We Play

What We Play

We like to think of our golf events as tournaments with a minimum $5 entry fee. That $5 goes to an Individual Low Net competition within your flight (everything is within your flight). Then there are several optional competitions you can get into if you're feeling good about your game. We all do, don't we? We all think that the next time we'll light it up. But you can get away with only a $5 investment. Here are the competitons you can enter. If you get into every one, it would be $15. They are all within your flight, except for the two Hole-in-One competitions - one for a $5 entry fee and the other is free.i>.

  • $5 - Low Net (the only one that is not optional)
  • $2 - Closest to the Pin on the Par 3 holes.
  • $3 - Gross Skins
  • $5 - Low Total Gross

  • Hole-in-One
    • $5 - Hole-in-One
      This would be only after someone makes a Hole-in-One and wins the whole (in one) pot - then it's another $5 if you want to stay in. These sometimes amount to over $1000 - something to think about even if you've never had one.
    • Free - Hole-in-one
      There is another Hole-in-One competition. You don't pay to get into this one - everybody is in. It is funded by taking the "odd dollars" from the other competitions and adding it to this one. For example, if the last $5 in Low Net is shared between two golfers, they each get $2 and the extra $1 goes into this Hole in One fund. Such a deal.

Based on your handicap, you will be slotted into a flight with players that have similar handicaps. Flights are typically 12-16 players although this may vary depending on the number of players signed up. You will play in a threesome or foursome, usually with players in the same flight although this is not always the case. You may ask to play with friends who may be in the same or a different flight. These flights usually play a set of tees which average around 6000 yards.

There are also Senior Flights (one or more) which consists of players over the age of 80 or, for some other reason, need to play the forward (shorter) tees. Total yardage for these flights tends to be around 5000 yards +- 200 yards.

Why We Play

We're really a great bunch of guys to play golf with, but besides that, you get to play some of the best courses in the area without having to make your own tee times, organize your own low $$$ event, or find friends to play with - you will make lots of friends with the Grumpy Old Men (we're not really all that grumpy).
