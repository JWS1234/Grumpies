The Courses We Play

The Grumpy Old Men Golf Club plays a number of different courses in Northeast Tarrant County, Eastern Dallas County, and Southern Denton County. The two tables below show (1) course metrics and (2) location and contact information. Clicking on the course name will take you to that course's website.

It should be pointed out that at the beginning of each year (or the end of the previous year), courses and dates are renegotiated. So this list may not always be current - some courses may not be on the schedule for the coming year and others may be new and not yet in this list.
