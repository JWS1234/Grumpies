Double Eagles

More impressive than a Hole in One, and much more rare. A double eagle, sometimes called an albatross, is a Hole in One on a par 4 or a 2 on a par 5. In all the years we've been keeping records, only one double eagle has been recorded.

Mike Gooden Hole #13 @ Tierra Verde - May 9, 2012
