The Everies

Some members of The Grumpy Old Men Golf Club are so dedicated to the game of golf (and maybe have little else to do on Mondays and Wednesdays) that they don't want to miss a day -- SO -- they have put themselves on a list of players that have committed to playing golf every Monday and/or every Wednesday. For this dedication to the club, they are automatically confirmed in those events before they are opened for general sign-up.

If you are new to the club, you may be surprised that there will be 40 or so "confirmed" players for an event before it is OPEN. This is why. You will also notice that they are "preflighted" - that is, assigned to a flight before we know what the mix of handicaps is for that day. This usually works but when a flight is overcrowded, somone may get moved to a different flight.
