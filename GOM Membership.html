Who Are the Grumpy Old Men?

The following table shows the Golf Profile of the members of the Grumpy Old Men. There is also a Personal Profile which contains personal information such as address, phonenumber, email address, etc.

There are three flags at the front of each row - F1, F2, and F3. The meanings of these flags are:

  • F1: Y means this golfer is a smoker.
  • F2: Y means this golfer prefers not to play in the same group as a smoker.
  • F3: Y means this golfer prefers to play the forward tees.

After the golfer's name and index are some more flags.

  • Every MON: Y means this golfer plays in nearly all Monday events.
  • Every WED: Y means this golfer plays in nearly all Wednesday events.
  • Low Gross: N means this golfer does not want to be entered in the optional Low Gross competition.
  • CTP: N means this golfer does not want to be entered in the optional Closest to the Pin competition.
  • Skins: N means this golfer does not want to be entered in the optional Skins competition.
  • Senior Flight: Y means this golfer is over 80 and prefers to play in a flight with other 80+ golfers.
